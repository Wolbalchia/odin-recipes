Pizza

Description

This is a recipe that has been passed down in my family for ages! I hope it fills your souls with its cheesy goodness as it has mine all these years.

Ingredients

  • 5 cups flour
  • 12 eggs
  • Jar marinara sauce
  • Pepperonies
  • Pineapples

Steps

  1. Mix flour, eggs, water in bowl
  2. Roll out dough
  3. Add cheese and toppings of choice
  4. Place in oven for 17 minutes at 350 degrees
  5. Enjoy!
